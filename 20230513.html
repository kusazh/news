Fight to Win, Forge Exemplary Conduct

“Other dogs you see would make you think they are adorable. These two dogs only reminded me of... ‘Fight to win, forge exemplary conduct’,” said Chinese comic Li Haoshi, whose stage name is House, during a stand-up performance in Beijing on May 13, 2023.

The punchline is part of the slogan that President Xi unveiled in 2013 as a goal for the Chinese military. Beijing authorities said Shanghai Xiaoguo Culture Media Co and Li had “humiliated the people's army”. They then confiscated 1.32m yuan of what was deemed to be illegal income, and fined the company another 13.35m yuan.
